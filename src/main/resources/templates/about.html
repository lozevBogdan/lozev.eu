About Me

Hi there! I'm Bogdan Lozev, a Geotechnical Engineer turned Software Engineer based in Sofia, Bulgaria. My journey in the world of technology began in the realm of geotechnical engineering, where I delved into the complexities of soil mechanics and foundation design.

Over time, I discovered the fascinating intersection between programming and geotechnical engineering. The ability to leverage technology to enhance efficiency and solve complex problems captivated me, leading to a shift in focus towards software development.

Today, I find myself fully immersed in the world of programming, where my passion for problem-solving and creative thinking has found a new home. As a full-time Software Engineer, I enjoy tackling challenges, writing elegant code, and continuously learning in this dynamic and ever-evolving field.
